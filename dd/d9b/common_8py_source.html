20  "Common" middleware for taking care of some basic operations:
21 
22  - Forbids access to User-Agents in settings.DISALLOWED_USER_AGENTS
23 
24  - URL rewriting: Based on the APPEND_SLASH and PREPEND_WWW settings,
25  this middleware appends missing slashes and/or prepends missing
26  "www."s.
27 
28  - If APPEND_SLASH is set and the initial URL doesn't end with a

31  urlpatterns, then an HTTP-redirect is returned to this new URL;
32  otherwise the initial URL is processed as usual.

34  - ETags: If the USE_ETAGS setting is set, ETags will be calculated from
35  the entire page content and Not Modified responses will be returned

37  """
